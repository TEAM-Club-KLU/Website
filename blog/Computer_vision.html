Introduction to Computer Vision

Computer Vision is a multidisciplinary field that enables computers to interpret, analyze, and understand visual information from the world. It involves developing algorithms and models that allow machines to extract meaningful insights from images and videos.

Computer Vision has applications in various domains, including image recognition, object detection, facial recognition, medical imaging, autonomous vehicles, and more. It plays a crucial role in enabling machines to perform tasks that typically require human visual perception.

Key Concepts in Computer Vision

Computer Vision involves a range of concepts and techniques:

  • Image Processing: Manipulating and enhancing images to extract useful information.
  • Feature Extraction: Identifying and extracting relevant features from images.
  • Object Detection: Locating and identifying objects within an image or video.
  • Image Classification: Categorizing images into predefined classes or categories.
  • Segmentation: Dividing an image into meaningful segments for analysis.
